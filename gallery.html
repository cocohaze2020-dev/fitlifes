Facility Overview

TRAINING ZONES

Cardio Area

Equipped with the latest treadmills, ellipticals, and stair climbers. Perfect for warming up or intense endurance tracking.

Legs Area

Dedicated heavy-duty squat racks, leg presses, and hack squats to build absolute lower-body power and foundations.

Biceps Area

A strict isolation zone featuring preacher curl benches, EZ-bars, and cable stations designed for peak arm contraction.

Triceps Area

Engineered for arm lockouts. Features dip stations, skull-crusher benches, and heavy cable pushdowns for horseshoe triceps.

Chest Area

Flat, incline, and decline Olympic benches paired with heavy dumbbell sections to maximize upper body push limits.

Back Area

Lat pulldowns, T-bar rows, and deadlift platforms. Everything you need to sculpt a strong, wide back.

Core & Abs Area

Decline crunch benches, medicine balls, and open turf space designed specifically for deep abdominal and core stability work.

Free Weights

A massive wall of calibrated dumbbells ranging from 5 lbs to 150 lbs. The pure iron temple for custom movements.

Comfort Room

Premium, clean, and modern locker rooms. Featuring high-pressure showers and secure lockers for pre- and post-workout prep.

Hydration Station

The front desk and lounge area where you can grab water, electrolyte drinks, and protein shakes before or after your session.
